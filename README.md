##  AI-Powered Recipe Finder 
This is a recipe search application designed to enhance your cooking experience by offering AI-driven recipe recommendations, automatically generating detailed cooking instructions with personalized optimizations.

###  Main Features 
路 Recipe Search: Quickly find recipes tailored to your ingredients and preferences.
路 AI-generated Instructions: generating detailed cooking instructions with your personalized dietary needs.
路 Favorites Management: Save your favorite recipes for easy access.
路 YouTube Integration: Automatically retrieves relevant cooking videos.
